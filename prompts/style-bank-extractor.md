# Style Bank Extractor Prompt

Analyze the target article and extract:
1) Title formula
2) Lead paragraph pattern
3) Heading hierarchy style
4) Paragraph length rhythm (short/medium/long mix)
5) Evidence style (facts/tables/links)
6) CTA style
7) FAQ style
8) Human-tone cues (what makes it feel editorial)

Output in concise bullets and reusable templates.
