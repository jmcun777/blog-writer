# Editorial Rubric (Pass >= 80/100)

Score every draft before publishing.

## 1) Information Density (25)
- 0-10: Generic, little concrete detail
- 11-18: Some concrete facts/tables/checklists
- 19-25: High-value specifics, decision-useful details

## 2) Human Readability (20)
- 0-8: Repetitive AI phrasing, robotic rhythm
- 9-15: Mostly readable, some templated feel
- 16-20: Natural editorial flow, varied sentence cadence

## 3) Structure Quality (20)
- 0-8: Weak hierarchy, unclear progression
- 9-15: Mostly clear but uneven transitions
- 16-20: Strong top-down logic and section coherence

## 4) Trust & Evidence (20)
- 0-8: Weak sourcing, over-assertive claims
- 9-15: Adequate sources, some uncertainty noted
- 16-20: Strong sources, explicit uncertainty boundaries

## 5) Conversion Utility (15)
- 0-5: No practical next step
- 6-10: Some guidance but weak actionability
- 11-15: Clear action checklist and decision support

## Hard fail rules
- Fabricated source/claim => FAIL
- Thin content (<1200 words unless explicitly allowed) => FAIL
- No practical checklist / no FAQ => FAIL
