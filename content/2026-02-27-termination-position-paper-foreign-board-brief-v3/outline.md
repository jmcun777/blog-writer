# Outline — Position Paper Format

1. Executive thesis and key takeaways
2. Legal-regulatory baseline (plain-language for foreign boards)
3. Strategic position: legal defensibility should outrank speed
4. Risk thesis by decision type
5. Policy stance: governance controls boards should mandate
6. Two illustrative board-level case contrasts
7. Secondary operational layer (SOP/checklist/exception handling)
8. System controls and audit trail expectations
9. Common governance failures and fixes
10. Board action agenda (next 30/60/90 days without D-xx framing)
11. What to watch next
12. FAQ
13. Disclaimer + Sources

Variation marker:
- Primary shape is position paper / policy argument (distinct from prior role-relay and cost-risk matrix runs).
