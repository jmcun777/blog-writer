# Outline — Checklist-Led Legal Primer Format

1. Key takeaways
2. Legal/regulatory primer (plain-language for foreign readers)
3. How to use this checklist
4. Master legal-first checklist (route, evidence, alignment, local confirmation, archive)
5. Two checklist application examples
6. Secondary operations section (SOP by role)
7. Exception handling checklist
8. System implementation checklist
9. Common mistakes and prevention
10. What to do now
11. What to watch next
12. FAQ
13. Disclaimer + Sources

Variation marker:
- Primary skeleton is checklist-led (different from prior decision-tree).
- No D-xx timeline framing as primary structure.
